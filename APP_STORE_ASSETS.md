## 1. APP ICON (1024×1024)

### Concept: Diamond Hands with Financial Edge

**Design Elements:**

**Primary Symbol:** 💎 Diamond
- Not emoji - clean geometric diamond shape
- Sharp facets catching light
- Represents "balls of steel" without being literal

**Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** Deep blue gradient (#0A2463 → #3E92CC) - Financial trust (Schwab)
- **Accent:** Neon cyan/electric blue (#00F0FF) - GTA Vice City glow
- **Highlight:** White/bright cyan for diamond facets - Premium feel
- **Background:** Dark navy (#0F1419) or dark gradient - Professional, hides in dark mode

**Style:**
- Clean geometric shapes (Uber minimalism)
- Sharp angles, no rounded softness
- Subtle neon glow around edges (GTA energy)
- Single focal point - the diamond
- Works at small sizes (watch, notifications)

**Layout:**
```
┌─────────────────────┐
│                     │
│                     │
│        💎          │   <- Diamond (geometric, not emoji)
│     ▓▓▓▓▓▓         │      Clean faceted shape
│   ▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓       │      Neon cyan highlights
│  ▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓      │      Deep blue gradient fill
│   ▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓       │      Dark navy background
│     ▓▓▓▓▓▓         │
│                     │
└─────────────────────┘
```

**Text:** NONE
- Icon is pure symbol - no text
- Diamond communicates "balls of steel" visually
- Clean, recognizable, memorable

**Alternative Concept (if diamond too generic):**
- Bull charging up arrow
- Arrow signal with volume bars
- VXX letters in neon
- Steel/chrome sphere with reflections

**What to avoid:**
- ❌ Emoji-style graphics (not professional)
- ❌ Too many elements (cluttered at small size)
- ❌ Text on icon (hard to read)
- ❌ Literal balls (not App Store appropriate)
- ❌ Stock chart lines (every trading app does this)
