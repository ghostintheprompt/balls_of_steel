## ⚠️ RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: App Store Rejection
**Concern:** Trading app might get scrutinized

**Mitigation:**
- Position as educational tool
- Clear disclaimers throughout
- No automated trading
- No profit promises
- User executes all trades

### Risk 2: Support Burden
**Concern:** Users expect live trading support

**Mitigation:**
- Comprehensive in-app help
- FAQ page (90% of questions)
- Email only, 3-5 day response
- Set expectations upfront

### Risk 3: Low Conversion Rate
**Concern:** Free users don't unlock

**Mitigation:**
- 7-day full trial (let them experience value)
- Institutional Flow as free (best strategy)
- Clear unlock benefits
- Prompt Coach shows immediate value

### Risk 4: Regulatory Issues
**Concern:** Trading advice = regulated

**Mitigation:**
- Educational tool positioning
- Not financial advice disclaimers
- No direct trade execution
- User makes all decisions
